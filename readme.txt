Convert numbers to words with our calculator. It accepts decimals and scientific notation and can display any U.S. dollar amount in words.

[https://www.calculator.io/numbers-to-words-converter/](https://www.calculator.io/numbers-to-words-converter/)

== Usage ==

Add the Numbers to Words Converter shortcode to your page, post or sidebar:

`[ci_numbers_to_words_converter]`

Add the following code to your template where you would like the Numbers to Words Converter to appear:

`<?php display_ci_numbers_to_words_converter(); ?>`

== Installation ==

1. Upload the Numbers to Words Converter /ci_numbers_to_words_converter/ folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the Numbers to Words Converter plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
